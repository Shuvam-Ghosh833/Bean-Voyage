About us


JAKE TYLER - Founder



    Since our humble beginnings in 1950, BEAN VOYAGE has been on a remarkable journey, evolving from a small coffee corner to a distinguished haven for coffee connoisseurs. The story of BEAN VOYAGE is a testament to our enduring commitment to quality, passion for coffee, and dedication to creating an exceptional experience for our patrons.

    In the early days, our founder, JAKE TYLER, a true coffee aficionado, envisioned a space where people could gather to enjoy the finest coffee in a welcoming atmosphere. JAKE TYLER started with a modest coffee shop, carefully selecting beans and perfecting brewing techniques to create a cup of coffee that stood out from the rest.

    As the decades unfolded, BEAN VOYAGE became a local favorite, cherished for its commitment to quality and the unique flavors found in every cup. The 1960s saw the introduction of signature blends, each one a symphony of meticulously roasted beans, setting the stage for our reputation as purveyors of exceptional coffee.

    The 1980s marked a period of expansion for BEAN VOYAGE, as our passion for coffee resonated with an ever-growing community. New locations were opened, each designed to capture the essence of our commitment to excellence. The introduction of single-origin coffees in the 1990s further enriched our offerings, allowing patrons to embark on a global journey of flavors without leaving their favorite coffee spot.

    In the early 2000s, BEAN VOYAGE embraced sustainability as a core value. Recognizing the importance of ethical sourcing, we established direct relationships with coffee farmers, ensuring fair practices and supporting communities at the source. This commitment to sustainability extended to eco-friendly initiatives within our shops, reinforcing our dedication to making a positive impact on the environment.

    As we entered the 21st century, BEAN VOYAGE embraced technology to enhance the customer experience. Online platforms allowed us to connect with a broader audience, while mobile ordering and loyalty programs provided convenience and added value for our patrons.

    Today, BEAN VOYAGE stands as a beacon of excellence in the coffee industry. With a rich history spanning over seven decades, we continue to evolve, innovate, and redefine the coffee experience. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community remains unwavering, inviting new generations to join us in celebrating the art of coffee.
